Why is it important to make schedules of the day?


Planning a daily routine and following it makes a person disciplined, develops organization and purposefulness. As a result, a mode of life is also developed, in which time and energy costs for non-essential things are minimized.

What is the daily routine?


The daily routine is a well-thought-out schedule of actions for the day, time planning for the purpose of its rational and most effective distribution.

How did famous scientists, composers and other bright heads spend their normal working day?


Kafka is one of the 161 inspiring heroes of the book "Genius Mode" by Mason Curry - bright minds, writers and writers, painters and scientists who maneuver in the daily routine and find their rhythm of life to do what they love. Someone falls asleep early, someone works at night, others prefer long walks or reinforce their energy with coffee and a donut.
